Why It Works?

Ferosom combines liposomal delivery with our proprietary LCE Coat. Liposomal delivery is an advanced technology that transports the iron directly to your cells in the intestine rather than the stomach. This ensures precise iron delivery so you can take a pass on the common side effects like stomach pain, constipation, and nausea.
